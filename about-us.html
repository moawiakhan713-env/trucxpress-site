Our Story

Why TrucXpress Exists

Too many carriers get burned — upfront fees for empty promises, forced loads that don't pay, double-brokered freight that vanishes along with the money. We built TrucXpress to be the opposite of all of that.

We're not just another dispatch company. We're a carrier-first, commission-only team built to protect your time, your truck, and your trust. Whether you're an owner-operator or managing a fleet, our services scale with your needs — from broker vetting to load tracking and compliance support.

You keep full control over which loads you accept. We present vetted options based on your preferences — lanes, rates, equipment type — and you make the final call. Always.

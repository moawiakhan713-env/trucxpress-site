FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How does commission-only dispatching work?
We operate on a performance-based model — you only pay a percentage of the loads we successfully book for you. No upfront fees, no monthly subscriptions. This keeps your costs low and ensures we're motivated to find the best-paying, most reliable freight for your business.
What documents do I need to start?
Just the basics: your MC authority, W-9, and certificate of insurance. We collect everything, handle your carrier setup packets, and get you dispatch-ready fast.
Do you work with new authorities?
Absolutely. We proudly support new carriers — helping you find brokers who work with new MCs, build your reputation, and secure consistent freight from day one.
Can I choose my own loads?
Always. You have full control over which loads you accept. We present vetted options based on your preferences — lanes, rates, equipment type — and you make the final call.
